About Our Scheme

We are planning to introduce parking space sharing management system (ParkSpace) to more effectively control parking and enhance parking environment for everyone. Purpose of online parking management system is to reserve a parking space online for the customers. The parking on campus can be an issue for staff, students and visitors alike. The vehicle park management system will enable us to make more efficient use of available spaces by controlling vehicle access at key entrances.
